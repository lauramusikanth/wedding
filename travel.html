Things To Do in Atlanta

Some places around the city you may want to explore if you have time to spare.

Georgia Aquarium

  • One of the largest aquariums in the world
  • Whale sharks, manta rays, and marine exhibits

World of Coca-Cola

  • Atlanta's most iconic brand
  • Taste Coca-Cola products from around the world

Atlanta Botanical Garden

  • Beautiful seasonal displays
  • Perfect for a relaxed afternoon

High Museum of Art

  • Atlanta's premier art museum
  • Modern, classic, and traveling exhibitions

Ponce City Market

  • Food hall, shopping, and rooftop attractions
  • One of Atlanta's most popular gathering places

Atlanta BeltLine

  • Walkable trails connecting neighborhoods
  • Restaurants, murals, and local culture

Atlanta History Center

  • Gardens, exhibits, and the Swan House
  • One of Atlanta's hidden gems

SkyView Atlanta

  • Ferris wheel overlooking downtown
  • Great skyline views

The Battery Atlanta

  • Dining, entertainment, and Braves baseball
  • Excellent nightlife option

Virginia-Highland

  • Walkable neighborhood with local charm
  • Restaurants, coffee shops, and boutiques

Piedmont Park

  • Atlanta's central urban park
  • Walking trails and skyline views

MLK National Historical Park

  • Birth home and historic sites of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.
  • One of Atlanta's most significant landmarks
