Our Story

Three years of choosing each other.

It started pretty simply. One night, a Tinder match. A lot of conversations. An ice cream date. And two people who somehow lived thirty minutes apart but hadn't met yet.

Then came the summer

Shannon thought we would probably be friends. That did not last very long. One hangout turned into another, and then most days together.At twenty-three, that seemed completely reasonable.

Young, happy, and very sure we were normal about each other.

The kind of summer that accidentally became the beginning of everything.

Cat parenting entered the chat.

The household manager.

Building together

Graduations, jobs, moves, Georgia, more school, new goals. We never held each other back. We just kept pushing each other forward.

Adventures, usually with snacks.

A lot of new things.

Proof that museums count as romance.

Finding pretty places everywhere we go.

The extraordinary ordinary. Boat days, cloudy skies, and doing life together.

Date nights.

Every new chapter somehow made more sense together.

Home has always been a person.

Somewhere along the way

we started building a life that felt like home.

Still growing. Still laughing. Still choosing each other.
